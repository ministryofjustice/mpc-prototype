Assess a case for early allocation

Use this service to assess whether the community probation team should take responsibility for this person's case early.

There are 3 possible outcomes:

  • eligible - this case will go to the community probation team early
  • discretionary - the community probation team will make a decision
  • not eligible - this case does not need to go to the community probation team early

Not all assessments will be sent

Eligible and discretionary referrals will only be sent to the community probation team if the person has 18 months or less until their release from prison.

If the person has more than 18 months until their release from prison, this assessment will be saved but not sent to the community probation team.

We will remind the allocated Prison Offender Manager (POM) to make a new assessment 18 months before release.
