How referrals are made

Cases with less than 18 months until release

Eligible and discretionary assessments will be sent to the community probation team.

Cases with over 18 months until release

Assessments will be saved but not sent to the community probation team.

Cases with any eligible or discretionary saved assessments might be eligible for early allocation.

We will remind the allocated POM to make a new assessment 18 months before release. The POM can view saved assessments.
