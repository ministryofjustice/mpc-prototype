POM sharing case details with COM

The POM should start sharing information about the case with the COM, highlighting any pre-release needs they have identified.

For Probation Service cases, the POM should be preparing to attend the handover meeting. POMs handing over legacy CRC cases must complete a POM to COM handover report.
